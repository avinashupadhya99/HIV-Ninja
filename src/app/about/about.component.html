About HIV Ninja

We are focused on providing support to HIV positive patients and help them along their journey.
We want to empower such patients through our platform.

Patients can signup on our website and meet other patients who are going through the same phase as them.
We aim to bring more awareness about the disease and educate people regarding what can be done about it.

Here's how the application works

  • Login to the application using an email. You will receive an OTP for passwordless authentication

  • Update your profile when you login for the first time.

  • Use the Support Group to interact with other patients.

  • Get the help you need.
